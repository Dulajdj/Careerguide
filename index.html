Powerful AI-Driven Features

Everything you need to succeed in your career journey

Component 01
AI Resume

Resume & Career Personalization

Enter your personal, academic, and work details and let AI craft professional CVs and cover letters tailored for specific roles. Get real-time job and internship suggestions based on your background and current market trends.

  • AI-generated ATS-friendly CVs & cover letters
  • Real-time job & internship recommendations
  • GitHub Project Detector — auto-enhance CV from your repos
Component 02
Smart Filtering

AI Applicant Filter

Practice interviews using your voice with real-time speech-to-text analysis and instant feedback. Ensures fair, unbiased assessments and holds job-specific quizzes, automatically sending results to relevant companies.

  • Voice-based interview practice with live feedback
  • Subject knowledge evaluation & bias-free scoring
  • Job-specific quiz results forwarded to employers
Component 03
Smart Guidance

Career & Learning Guidance

The Smart Personal Development System analyses your skills and goals against live job market data, detects skill gaps through quiz results and GitHub projects, and recommends targeted courses and training videos to accelerate your growth.

  • Personalised career path recommendations
  • Trending roles & in-demand skills analysis
  • Curated online courses & training resources
Component 04
Interview AI

Interview Preparation Assistant

Choose your target job role and interview type, and the AI generates tailored interview questions on demand. Respond by voice or text — the system scores your answers and delivers actionable feedback to sharpen your performance.

  • AI-generated role-specific interview questions
  • Voice & text answer analysis with scoring
  • Detailed feedback to improve weak areas

About AI Career Guide

Bridging the gap between talent and opportunity in Sri Lanka.

Empowering Your Professional Growth

Navigating the modern job market can be challenging. AI Career Guide is a comprehensive ecosystem designed specifically to elevate job seekers and streamline recruitment processes.

By leveraging advanced Large Language Models (LLMs), our platform provides an end-to-end career toolkit. From generating ATS-friendly smart resumes and mapping out personalized learning paths, to conducting real-time, voice-analyzed mock interviews—we ensure you are fully equipped to land your dream role.
